## Migration Impact Assessment

**Low complexity:**
- Blog posts (20) - small volume, clean data, all have SEO metadata
- Pages (30) - need Divi content extraction but limited count
- Forms (2) - simple field structures, easy to recreate
- Redirects (14) - small volume, plus product/category URL redirects to generate

**Medium complexity:**
- Non-linen products (~537) - clean data, most have images, straightforward import
- Navigation menus - complex hierarchy but well-documented
- SEO metadata - Yoast data available for all content

**High complexity:**
- Linen products (6,140) - massive volume, minimal images, need consolidation strategy
- Quote/estimate workflow - core business function, needs Shopify app + custom template logic
- Product URL redirects - 6,677 products changing from /product/ to /products/ pattern
- Blog URL redirects - date-based WP URLs to Shopify /blogs/news/ pattern
